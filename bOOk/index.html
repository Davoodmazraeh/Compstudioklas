About The Product

With bOOk, all your children books are in one place and we are here to empower the parents and give them the necessary information to support their children and improve their literacy from young age.

A Million Word Gap

Children who has been read five books a day will enter kindergarten with 1.4M words more than the ones who never been read to

As digital media is a part of children reading habits today, parents are concerned about some aspects of digital media that they can not control, hence we have established a system to improve children literacy while giving more control to parents to support their children with bOOk

  • Create a safe space for children to read digital books of their own
  • Using the data to help parents make better and more informed decisions
  • Increasing the efficiency of the parent/child interactions
  • Having all your children books in one place

Through exploratory narrative, the stories will be delivered to your child in an interactive manner with the aim to improve their literacy rather than focus on gamificaion of those stories. The novel words in each story are put based on the age range of the book reader choses. The choices of books will be through parents based on children reading habits and their informed decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Some of our user's frequently asked questions

  • Does this product limit my involvement with my child?

    No, this product is designed to increase your interactions with your children while using digital books to ultimately make more informed decision on what reading materials are consumed by their children.

  • Can we use one bOOk for all of our children?

    Although this is possible, we recommend you choose one bOOk for each child, since this is a personalized device and will help you with the data regarding each child and it is unique to that child alone.

  • How many books can we add to library of each device?

    All books are curated and on the cloud, so as soon as you purchased the book, you will have unlimited access to the books you hvae in your library without any worry to lose or delete them.

  • Do we have to import the books into the device?

    All the books can be directly purchased through the device and will be immediately ready to use. These books are curated and unique to bOOk.

  • Do we have to connect our app to bOOk to be able to transfer the books after purchase?

    Every book you purchase will be synched automatically to your children bOOk device, unless you aim to keep it from synching and surprise them at your own pace.

  • How many accounts can we link to the Parent's App?

    You can connect every bOOk device you have to one Parent's App so you can view all the information in one app, also you can connect your app to your spouse app so you can have a synchronized information on the books and children's progression.
